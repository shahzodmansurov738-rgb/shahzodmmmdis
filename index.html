ABOUT US




Times School is the first innovative learning center in Tashkent, where highly qualified teachers with effective teaching methods await you. With us, you can prepare for exams with high quality, train your staff in a foreign language.

In the 5 years of our center's existence, we have successfully graduated over 5000 students. Our graduates have been admitted to prestigious universities abroad and in Uzbekistan. Our branches are located in the most convenient locations.

Times School is popular not only among schoolchildren and applicants but also among preschoolers and adults, including corporate clients. This is exactly the place where you will be helped to master any subject, regardless of your age.
